About me

My focus is to develop projects that have something to add to people's lives. The story of how I became a developer is simple and kinda funny; I've always used a lot of technology since I was a kid, and I decided that I would become a developer without even knowing exactly what a developer did. From that moment, I started a technical course, did an internship as a Front-end Developer, and the rest is history.

Nowadays, I have experience in a little bit of everything that involves a website, including technical and non-technical areas. I have experience in creating and configuring servers, developing pixel-perfect frontend based on layout, backend development focused on business logic, and also experience in managing teams and clients.

Lately, I have been focusing more on understanding 'why' and 'how' some things work, and I have also been exercising thinking 'out of the box' to deliver a unique experience to the customer, so that they have something to add, not just deliver a website or functionality without worrying about who/how/why will use it. Challenges keep me motivated.
